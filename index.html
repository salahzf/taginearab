TAGINE Maroccan




Definition of TAGINE maroccain

One of the popular foods known in Morocco is the Moroccan casserole,
a delicious and delicious cuisine, which is constantly on the Moroccan trip and abound in the moments,
and the casserole is a pot made of pottery,
and what distinguishes it is able to preserve the nutritional values of cooked items,
and also gives food A distinctive flavor does not appear in any type of dishes prepared in the usual pots,
and Moroccan casserole is easy to prepare and its amounts are available constantly,
especially that the casserole can be made in more than one way and different amounts,
and we will present the way of the Moroccan chicken casserole.

TAGINE ingredients


-- Chicken cut into four pieces.
-- Two onions cut into slices.
 -- 2 pieces of sliced tomatoes.
 -- Three cloves of garlic.
-- Teaspoon ground ginger.
-- A quarter spoon of saffron.
-- Five tablespoons vegetable oil.
-- spoon teaspoon ground cinnamon.
-- Sprinkle black pepper.
-- Salt, to tasteCasserole

METHOD PREPAR TAGINE

== Wash the chicken pieces well, then put in the casserole and pour water.
 == Put the casserole over medium heat, and leave for a quarter of an hour.
 == Add salt, saffron, onion slices, tomato slices, cinnamon, ginger and black pepper to the casserole.
 == Leave for an hour
== Serve the tagine in its form
